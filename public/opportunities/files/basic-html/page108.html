MakerDAO has evolvedsignificantly over the years, incorporating real-world assets as collateral and implementinga governance-controlled savings rate that pays interest to DAI holders.Key Concept: Flash Loans %u2014 DeFi's Most Unique InnovationA flash loan lets you borrow millions of dollars from Aave with zero collateral, on the conditionthat you repay it within the same Ethereum transaction. If you don't repay, the entiretransaction reverts as if it never happened. Legitimate uses include arbitrage (borrow, trade ontwo exchanges at different prices, repay, keep the spread), liquidations (borrow to liquidate anundercollateralised position, collect the bonus, repay), and collateral swaps (replace onecollateral type with another atomically). Attack uses include price oracle manipulation. Flashloans exist only in DeFi, there is no equivalent in traditional finance.Yield Strategies: The Art of Putting Capital to WorkYield in DeFi comes from multiple sources, and understanding their origin, real economicactivity versus token emissions, is the most important skill in navigating DeFi as a financialparticipant.Lending yield is the most straightforward form: deposit an asset in a lending protocol andearn interest paid by borrowers. The yield reflects genuine demand to borrow the asset.When demand to borrow USDC is high, because traders want leverage, becausearbitrageurs need short-term capital, because businesses need working capital, lendingrates rise. When demand is low, rates fall. This yield is real in the sense that it is generatedby economic activity, not by the protocol inflating its token supply.Liquidity provision yield is the fee income earned by AMM liquidity providers. Every swapin a Uniswap pool generates a fee, distributed to liquidity providers proportional to theirshare of the pool. High-volume trading pairs, ETH/USDC, BTC/ETH, major stablecoin pairs,generate meaningful fee income. Lower-volume pairs generate little. This yield is also real,it comes from traders paying for price discovery and execution, but it must be weighedagainst the impermanent loss risk described earlier.Staking yield comes from securing Proof of Stake networks. Staking ETH on the Ethereumvalidator set earns approximately three to five percent annually, funded by new ETHissuance and transaction fees.
