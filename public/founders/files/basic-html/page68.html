                                    THE FOUNDER'S GUIDE TO BUILDING IN REGULATED MARKETSDigital assets role: The NDIC%u2019s relevance to digital asset businesses is indirect butgrowing: as the CBN begins to allow licensed banks to offer digital asset custody andsettlement services, the question of whether digital asset deposits are covered by theNDIC%u2019s deposit insurance scheme becomes consequential for consumer protectionframeworks.Founder watch-out: Monitor NDIC guidance if your product involves any form ofdeposit-taking or fiat custody in conjunction with digital asset services. The boundarieshere are still being drawn.NCCNigerian CommunicationsCommissionMinistry / Oversight: Federal Ministry of Communications, Innovation and DigitalEconomyCore mandate: Regulation of telecommunications infrastructure and services.Digital assets role: Relevant to digital asset businesses that rely on USSD, SMS, or mobilenetwork infrastructure, common in financial inclusion products serving unbanked users.The NCC%u2019s licensing framework applies to any platform that constitutes a 'value addedservice' on mobile networks.Founder watch-out: If your digital asset product is delivered through mobile networkinfrastructure, USSD codes, SMS-based wallets, SIM-linked accounts, you may requireNCC engagement in addition to your SEC and CBN obligations. Fintech products servingthe mass market frequently operate in the intersection of NCC and CBN jurisdiction.CACCorporate AffairsCommissionMinistry / Oversight: Federal Ministry of Industry, Trade and InvestmentCore mandate: Registration of companies, business names, and incorporated trustees.Digital assets role: Every VASP seeking SEC Nigeria registration must first be incorporatedat the CAC. The CAC also maintains the Beneficial Ownership Register, a key componentof Nigeria%u2019s FATF compliance reforms.Founder watch-out: CAC registration is the administrative prerequisite for everythingelse. Ensure your corporate structure, share capital, shareholders, directors, is correctlyconstituted for the regulatory landscape you are entering. SEC Nigeria%u2019s requirements forlocal incorporation, Nigerian CEO residency, and board composition must be reflected inyour CAC registration.The Overlap Problem %u2014 and How to Navigate ItThe most challenging regulatory situations founders face are not the ones where a singleregulator has clear jurisdiction. They are the ones where multiple regulators haveoverlapping or conflicting jurisdiction over the same product.A stablecoin payment product in Nigeria is the clearest example. The stablecoin itself is adigital asset under ISA 2025, SEC Nigeria%u2019s territory. The naira conversion mechanism is apayment system, CBN%u2019s territory. If the stablecoin is denominated in naira, it mayimplicate monetary policy, CBN%u2019s territory again.
