                                    THE FOUNDER'S GUIDE TO BUILDING IN REGULATED MARKETSNigeria's apex capital markets regulator, established under the Investments and Securities Act. SEC Nigeria isresponsible for regulating the Nigerian capital market, protecting investors, and maintaining marketintegrity. Following ISA 2025, SEC Nigeria is the primary regulator for all digital asset activities in Nigeria %u2014including VASP licensing, digital asset offerings, exchanges, and custodians.SEC Nigeria's Digital Asset Rules (2022, amended 2024, further amendments effective June 2025) are theprimary operational framework for VASP licensing. The ARIP programme is SEC Nigeria's main tool foronboarding digital asset businesses. Founders must understand the distinction between SEC Nigeria'sjurisdiction (securities and investment activities) and the CBN's jurisdiction (payment, banking, monetarypolicy).See also: ISA 2025, ARIP, VASP, DAX, DAOP, DAC, CBNSecurity TokenA digital token that represents an investment contract %u2014 i.e., a financial security %u2014 such as equity in acompany, a debt instrument, a share of revenue, or a fractional interest in a real asset. Security tokens arethe digital equivalent of traditional securities and are subject to full securities regulation wherever they areissued or traded.Under Nigeria's ISA 2025, all virtual assets are classified as securities. Tokens that carry investmentcharacteristics (profit participation, dividend rights, capital appreciation) will be treated as security tokenssubject to SEC Nigeria's full regulatory framework.See also: ISA 2025, Utility Token, Governance Token, RWA, ICOSeed PhraseA human-readable sequence of 12 or 24 random words generated when a non-custodial wallet is created.The seed phrase is the master backup of the wallet's private keys: anyone who holds the seed phrase canrecover the wallet and all its assets on any compatible device. Seed phrases must be written down andstored securely offline %u2014 they should never be shared, photographed, or stored digitally.See also: Private Key, Non-Custodial Wallet, Cold WalletSmart ContractSelf-executing code deployed on a blockchain that automatically enforces the terms of an agreement whenpredefined conditions are met %u2014 without the need for intermediaries. Smart contracts are immutable(cannot be altered once deployed) and transparent (their code is publicly visible). They are the foundationalbuilding blocks of DeFi, DAOs, NFTs, and tokenisation. Bugs in smart contract code can result in significantfinancial losses and cannot be easily reversed.%u2014 220 %u2014
