                                    THE FOUNDER'S GUIDE TO BUILDING IN REGULATED MARKETSthe tokenisation of a beneficial interest in a land-holding SPV, may require gubernatorialconsent under the Land Use Act.This is not a theoretical concern. Nigerian courts have in multiple cases held thattransactions involving land interests conducted without the required governor%u2019s consentare void. A tokenised real estate project whose underlying SPV holds a Certificate ofOccupancy, and whose tokens represent interests in that SPV, may be conducting whatamounts to a transfer of land interests, one for each token transaction, without individualgovernor%u2019s consent.The practical approach that most experienced Nigerian real estate tokenisationpractitioners adopt is to structure the SPV%u2019s interest as a beneficial interest rather than alegal interest in the land, and to obtain a general governor%u2019s consent for the SPV structureat the outset, covering the issuance and secondary trading of tokens. But this approach hasnot yet been definitively tested in Nigerian courts, and any founder building in this spaceshould obtain a specific legal opinion on the title and consent structure from a practitionerwith verified experience in both Nigerian land law and digital asset securities regulation.The Disputed Title RiskNigerian real estate title disputes are not rare. A significant proportion of premium properties inLagos, Abuja, and other major cities carry some form of title uncertainty, overlapping claims,disputed inheritance, fraudulent Certificate of Occupancy issuance. The cost of discovering a titledispute after tokens have been issued to investors is catastrophic: the asset%u2019s value is frozen,redemptions cannot be processed, and token holders have a claim against an asset that cannot becleanly sold. Title due diligence for any Nigerian real estate tokenisation project must be exhaustiveand independently verified by a qualified property lawyer. It is the most important, and mostfrequently underinvested, component of the project.The Custody and Title ChainThe second inseparable component of RWA tokenisation is the custody and title chain, thedocumented, verified, auditable sequence of legal arrangements that connects theinvestor%u2019s token to the underlying asset at every point in the product%u2019s life.%u2014 107 %u2014
